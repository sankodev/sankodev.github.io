Reel Game is a company out of Troy, Michigan; a small mid-west town that carries a large passion for fishing. Whether we fish the fresh water Great Lakes, or ice fishing in the winter, we bring the highest class of fishing sport available.

Our Local Start Up

We choose to focus most of our time towards what's important; spending time out on the lake fishing. Our principals are to avoid endorsing our company to the oversourced crowd. Our humble roots keep us searching for the path we want with each hand-crafted fishing reel we present at local fishing & hunting events. Our company holds together our community of followers who share the same goal of local fishery ideals. We keep our family community of neighbors and fellow fishers close to continue our mission.
