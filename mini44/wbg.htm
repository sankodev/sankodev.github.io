    The Calgary, Alberta Family
    and Home to our Calgary Flames

    We believe that whiskey holds a warm hearty compassion in a drink and we pride ourselves on presenting our family distilled whiskey & bourbon to the great city of Calgary. WBG dishes re-introduce a home-styled savory taste to Canadian roots. Located next to the Scotiabank Saddledrome Arena, we welcome the Flames' family into our home. Exclusive part-owner and Flames' headcoach, Bob Hartley, approached us on openening week and is now a proud share-holder to WBG. A taste of the gritty back-woods city with a drop of liquor awaits your aftergame nights.
