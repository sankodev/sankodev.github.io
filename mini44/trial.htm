    Our Alberta Family
    and Home to the Calgary Flames

    We believe that whiskey builds rich culture, holds a warm hearty compassion in a drink, and we pride ourselves on presenting our family distilled whiskey & Vodka mixes to the great city of Calgary. VWT dishes re-introduce a home-styled savory taste to Canadian roots. Located next to the Scotiabank Saddledrome Arena, we welcome the Flames' family into our home. Exclusive part-owner and Flames' headcoach, Bob Hartley, approached us on openening week and is now a proud share-holder to VWT. A taste of the gritty back-woods city with a drop of liquor awaits your postgame nights.
